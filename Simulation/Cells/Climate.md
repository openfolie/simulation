## Cold
Polar (very cold, very dry)
Tundra (cold, low moisture)
Boreal (cold, moderate moisture)
## Temperate
Oceanic (mild, wet, low seasonality)
Continental (warm summers, cold winters)
Mediterranean (dry summers, wet winters)
## Dry
Arid Desert (hot, very dry)
Cold Desert (cold, dry)
Semi-arid / Steppe (low moisture but not extreme)
## Tropical
Tropical Rainforest (hot, very wet)
Tropical Monsoon (wet/dry cycles)
Tropical Savanna (wet + long dry season)
## Extreme / Special
Highland (temperature varies with elevation)
Coastal (heavily wind + moisture influenced)
